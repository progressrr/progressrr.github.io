FirstNet Online Banking

Technology is ever changing and at First Century we try our best to keep up with the latest banking trends.  Today, most banks are on-line, accessible 24-hours a day via the latest electronic technology and we are no different.  With FirstNET Online Banking, you can keep a close eye on all your accounts. Here are some of the great ways you can handle your finances online:

  • Check your account balance
  • Balance your statement  (previous month's statement included)
  • View & print your check images
  • Print your account statements
  • View account transactions
  • Transfer funds from accounts (in-bank only)
  • Bill Pay  --  Bill pay includes "Expedited Bill Pay" (applicable fees will be charged)
  • Pop Money   --  An easy way to send money via email or text

For further details or to get started with FirstNET Online Banking, contact your nearest office or call the Electronic Banking Coordinator at +1 (775) 309-4482.

 

First BankLine

You can enjoy 24-hour access to your account information with our FREE telephone banking service.  This great service lets you do your banking when you want, wherever you want. 

Call First BankLine Today
+1 (775) 309-4482

First BankLine is FREE and very easy to use.  Simply follow the step-by-step recorded menu to perform a variety of banking tasks.

  • Receive account balance information
  • Track checks or withdrawals
  • Track deposits
  • Create and change your own personal access code
  • Transfer funds between bank accounts

Please have your banking accounts number available.  You will be asked to enter them before you can use most features on the menu.

Safe Deposit Box

Are your valuables, under lock and key?  A safe deposit box can provide safe keeping for all of your important belongings.  Instead of taking chances, use a safe deposit box for security – and find peace of mind.

Items to keep in a safe deposit box may include:

  • Legal documents, titles, deeds, passports
  • Heirlooms, valuable mementos
  • Jewelry, precious metals, coins

A safe deposit box is your best insurance to protect against fire and theft.  Please keep in mind that box sizes and availability will vary at our different branch locations.

Visa Debit Card

The First Century Visa Debit Card is the safe, convenient way to pay for purchases from your personal checking or savings account without the hassle of writing checks.  It also gives you access to your account from any ATM.

This card functions like the ATM card but is enhanced with the Visa symbol. It is designed to replace cash and checks at the point of sale and may be used wherever you see the Visa symbol.

Just present your card at the time of purchase and the amount will automatically be deducted from your checking or savings account. You'll receive a receipt for easy record keeping and every transaction will be detailed on your monthly statement.

For lost or stolen Visa Check cards please call +1 (775) 309-4482

Credit Card
