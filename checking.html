First Interest Checking

First Interest Checking provides an option for our customers who want to keep their funds in an interest bearing account.

  • $1000 Minimum Balance
  • $10 Monthly Service Fee if Account Balance Falls Below Minimum Balance (This fee is waived if you have a $2,000 minimum balance in a qualifying savings account. Customer is required to pay for pre-printed checks.)
  • Interest is compounded daily and credited monthly

  • $16.25 Monthly Service Fee if Account Balance Falls Below Minimum Qualifying Deposit or Loan Balance
